Pixie-Bob

  • Medium to large in size and heavy cat that has a bobbed tail and are often polydactyl
  • Coat may be short or medium in length, and is thick, double-layered, and woolly in texture
  • Generally spotted, with small spots and heavily ticked hairs
  • Tail is usually between two inches long and to the hock, and is flexible but kinked or knotted
  • Often has more than five toes on one or both of its front paws and/or more than four toes on one or both of its back paws
  • active | calm | inquisitive | loyal | relaxed | social | strong

  • Type of Breed: natural
  • Size: large
  • Weight: 8.8 to 18 lb (pounds)
  • Hair Length: short
  • Shedding: seasonal
  • Grooming: moderate
  • Origin: United States
