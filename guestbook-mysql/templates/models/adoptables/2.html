Abyssinian

  • Medium-sized and muscular cat with a ticked coat and the general appearance of a mountain lion
  • Coat appears iridescent due to its agouti ticking, with bands of color on each hair
  • Also exists in a Somali variety, which has a longer coat and furnishings on its ears and tail
  • Large eyes are surrounded by dark "eyeliner" and then another ring of lighter color
  • active | affectionate | agenda-driven | busy | curious | intelligent | interactive | loyal | playful | social

  • hair length: medium | short
